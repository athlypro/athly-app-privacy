Privacy Policy

1. Summary

Athly Fitness ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Athly Fitness is designed as an offline-first, on-device application. We do not operate servers that collect, store, or process your personal fitness data. With the exception of third-party advertising services described in Section 5, your health and fitness information stays on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally On Your Device

When you use Athly Fitness, the following categories of information are created and stored exclusively on your device using standard device storage technologies (such as local storage and on-device databases):

  • Body metrics you choose to enter (e.g., weight, height, goal weight)
  • Workout history, exercise logs, and performance telemetry
  • Custom routines and training preferences
  • Session duration, estimated calorie data, and Rate of Perceived Exertion (RPE) entries
  • App settings and preferences

This information is not transmitted to us. We have no server-side database of your personal fitness information, and we cannot access, view, or recover this data on your behalf.

2.2 Information Collected by Third-Party Advertising

The App displays advertisements served through Google AdMob. To deliver and measure ads, AdMob and its partners may automatically collect certain technical information, which can include:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as your device's Advertising ID)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, general device settings)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address or device signals)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, engagement)

This data collection is performed by Google, not by Athly Fitness directly, and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Partner Policy. We do not provide AdMob or any advertising partner with your fitness logs, body metrics, workout history, or any other health-related data. Advertising data collection is entirely separate from the health data described in Section 2.1.

You may be able to limit ad personalization through your device's operating system settings (e.g., "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

3. How We Use Information

Because we do not collect or receive your personal fitness data, we do not use, analyze, sell, rent, or share it for any purpose, including marketing, analytics, or research.

Data collected by AdMob is used solely to serve and measure advertisements as described in Section 2.2.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal fitness data to any third party.
  • We do not transmit your health metrics, workout logs, or body data to remote servers, data brokers, or analytics providers.
  • The only third party integrated into the App is Google AdMob, for the limited purpose of displaying advertisements as described above.

5. Data Storage, Security & Retention

All fitness and health-related data is stored locally on your device using standard, industry-recognized storage APIs. Because this data never leaves your device:

  • We cannot access, back up, recover, or restore this data if it is lost (e.g., due to app deletion, device reset, or device loss).
  • The security of this data depends in part on your device's own security features (passcode, encryption, OS-level protections). We recommend keeping your device secure and your operating system up to date.
  • Data persists on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You retain full ownership and control over the data generated within the App. You may permanently delete your locally stored data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear Data" function within the App's Settings menu, or
  • Uninstalling the App from your device.

This deletion is permanent and irreversible. Because we maintain no remote backups or cloud copies of your fitness data, once deleted, this information cannot be recovered by you or by us.

7. Children's Privacy

Athly Fitness is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly design the App to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us using the information in Section 11 so we can address the matter.

8. International Users

Athly Fitness is available for use in multiple regions. Because your fitness data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted to us, it generally remains subject to the laws of the country in which your device is located, rather than being transferred internationally. Data collected by AdMob may be subject to Google's own international data handling practices, as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
